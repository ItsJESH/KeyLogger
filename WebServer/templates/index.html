$ How to Spot a Keylogger (a.k.a. Catch That Sneaky Rat)
Think you might have a keylogger? Here’s how to play digital detective
- 🐢 Your computer feels slow – like it just woke up from a nap and hasn't had coffee yet.
- 🧛‍♂️ Something’s running in the background, but you didn’t open it. Suspicious!
- 💾 Weird files or programs you don’t remember installing are just... there.
- 🌐 Internet acting strange – like sending secret messages behind your back.
- 🧠 You forgot your password, but someone else didn’t. Uh-oh... ☠️
In short: if your laptop feels haunted — it might be... 👻
$ How to Stop Keyloggers (Like a Cyber Ninja)
Don’t panic! Here’s how to keep your computer safe and sound,
- 🚫 Don’t click sketchy links, especially ones that scream “WIN A PS5” or “SEE WHO VISITED YOUR PROFILE” 🚗💀
- 🧼 Keep your software updated – updates = digital soap. Clean those bugs out!
- 🦠 Install a good antivirus – let the software do the ninja work.
- 🔐 Use 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication) – even if your password gets stolen, the hacker still needs your phone.
- 🧹 Check your Task Manager every now and then. If you see a process named “notavirus.exe”... yeah, it’s probably a it.
- 🧊 Use a password manager – so you don’t type passwords in the first place!
